About

Build-a-thon 2025 is an online hackathon taking place on November 7th through 10th, hosted by the Buildathon Foundation, a student-led organization empowering youth through computer science hackathons. Over 48 hours, participants build apps, games, or websites while attending beginner-friendly workshops, receiving mentorship, and competing real prizes. Industry professionals will evaluate submissions and finalists will present live.

Rules & Eligibility

  • Attendees from all backgrounds, genders, and geographies are welcome, subject to standard restrictions.
  • Age limit: 13–22 years old. Participants must meet regional legal requirements to receive prizes.
  • Committee members are not eligible to compete; they may mentor during the coding phase.
  • No country restrictions, but winners are responsible for any taxes on prize winnings and must be eligible to receive prizes.
  • Teams can have a maximum of four members. Each team may submit only one entry.
  • Participants may not join more than one team during the event.

Submission Details

Submissions are made via Devpost. Join our Discord for announcements and any schedule changes.

Requirements

  • All code must be written after the hackathon start time (November 7).
  • Use of external tools, libraries, and APIs is allowed (disclose where relevant).
  • Submissions will be judged on Impact, Creativity, and Technical Execution.
  • By the deadline, teams must submit: a 2–3 minute demo video and a public GitHub repository containing the code.
  • Finalists will present their product live on the announced date.

More specific submission format and judging criteria are available in the information document.
